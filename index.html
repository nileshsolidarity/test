Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the visa process take?

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Standard processing takes 5-7 days, Express takes 2-3 days, and Rush processing takes 24-48 hours.

What documents do I need?

Typically, you'll need a valid passport, passport-sized photos, and supporting documents depending on visa type (flight bookings, hotel reservations, invitation letters, etc.).

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption to protect your data. We're fully compliant with GDPR and other international data protection regulations.

What if my visa is rejected?

While we have a 98% success rate, in the rare case of rejection, we'll help you understand the reason and guide you on reapplication. Service fees may be partially refundable depending on the case.

Can I track my application?

Yes! You'll receive a tracking number and can check your application status 24/7 through our online portal or mobile app.
